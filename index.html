Our Story

Bike Haven was founded in 2010 by two lifelong cycling enthusiasts, Jane and Mike. After years of working in the cycling industry, they decided to strike out on their own and open a bike shop that was different from the rest.

From the beginning, Jane and Mike were committed to creating a space that was welcoming, inclusive, and focused on providing the best possible customer service. They wanted to offer more than just bikes and gear - they wanted to build a community of passionate cyclists who could share their love of cycling with others.

Over the years, Bike Haven has grown and evolved, but our commitment to excellence has remained the same. We're still dedicated to providing top-quality bikes and accessories, as well as expert advice and support for cyclists of all levels. We're still passionate about helping our customers find the perfect bike and gear to suit their needs, and we're still committed to building a community of enthusiastic and supportive riders.

So whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, we invite you to come be a part of the Bike Haven community. We'd love to share our love of cycling with you!

Our Services

At Bike Haven, we're dedicated to providing a range of services to meet all your cycling needs. Here are some of the services we offer:

  • Bike sales: We stock a wide range of bikes, from road bikes to mountain bikes, electric bikes, and more. Our experienced staff can help you find the perfect bike to suit your needs and preferences.
  • Bike rentals: If you're visiting the area or just want to try out a new type of bike, we offer bike rentals at affordable prices. Choose from our selection of high-quality bikes and explore the local trails and roads.
  • Bike repairs and maintenance: Our team of skilled mechanics can help keep your bike in top condition with regular maintenance and repairs. We offer everything from basic tune-ups to full overhauls.
  • Bike fittings: A proper bike fit is essential for comfort and performance. Our staff can help you find the right bike size and adjust it to fit your body perfectly.
  • Bike accessories: We offer a comprehensive range of accessories, including helmets, lights, locks, and clothing, to ensure that you're safe and comfortable on every ride.
  • Bike education and events: We're passionate about cycling and we love to share our knowledge with others. We offer classes and events on topics like bike maintenance, safe riding, and group riding skills.

Whatever your cycling needs, Bike Haven is here to help. Stop by and see us today!
